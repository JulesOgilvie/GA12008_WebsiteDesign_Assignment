About Me!

Background

Born and raised amidst the stunning landscapes of Scotland, I've always embraced change and diversity, having moved around quite a bit during my childhood. This journey introduced me to a mosaic of people and cultures across Scotland, each experience shaping my perspective and fueling my curiosity.

Today, I call Dundee home, where I thrive in my role as an apprentice service desk analyst, navigating the intricate world of technology with a unique vantage point shaped by my visual impairment.

Education

My educational path has been as colorful as the art I create. After dedicating three years to studying animation, I was captivated by the magic of bringing ideas to life through visuals.

This passion for creativity didn't stop there; I ventured into a one-month Code Clan course in web development, where I earned an HNC qualification.
